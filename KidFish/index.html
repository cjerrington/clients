About Kid Fish Lubbock

Kidfish Lubbock is a free event for you and your family and will be Saturday, April 16th, 2016, 7 a.m. â€“ 1 p.m. at Maxey Park. Free food will be provided with registration. The lake will be stocked with channel catfish ready for your family.

Free Event!

The main sponsor for KidsFish Lubbock is the Lubbock Lions Club and Cabella's.

What to expect?

A relaxing morning with your family teaching them about fishing and respecting the outdoors.

What do I need to bring?

The only thing you need to bring is your family and a fishing pole.

This will be our 4th annual KidsFish. We are thankful for our sponsors and the community who loves coming together.
