Texas Tech Word

Texas Tech Word is published by Word Publications each year for students of Texas Tech University.

The Word Magazine is distributed across campus and includes coupons, class information, school information, and much more.

They wanted a way to publish their stories in an online format allowing for advertising and more exposure to the wonderful coupons that are provided.

Project Details

  • WordPress design for ease of use today and in the future.
  • Design for a magazine and add features that attract and retain students on the site to find a new way to gain the same inforation.
  • Provide a way to disperse the coupons online for more access.
  • Google Analytics to better serve the students by knowing how they are arriving and what content they are looking for.
