Kid Fish Lubbock

Kid Fish Lubbock is sponsored by the Lubbock Lions Club.

Kids ages from 0-17 can sign up for a free day of fishing, food, activities and fun. This is a fun project to work on each year making sure kids of all ages know about fishing and can experience the fun of it all.

Project Details

  • Purchase domain and hosting
  • WordPress design for ease of use today and in the future.
  • Custom plugin for registration. Registrations are placed into a database and easily accessed from an administrative page once logged in.
